Requirements

Marley runs on 64-bit Linux platforms (Linux Mint, Ubuntu or Arch Linux). Nvidia, Intel, or AMD OpenGL graphics acceleration. Either X11- or Wayland-based desktops.

Controllers

Marley is control via wired or wireless connect game controller. Support includes WiiMote (& Motion Plus), DualShock 3 and 4, and Xbox. More support is planned.
